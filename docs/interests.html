UX/UI Prototyping and Design Work

Interaction Design

(1) SlideCraft - AI Powered Design Tool

SlideCraft is an AI-powered slide design tool created to simplify the presentation-making process for students and educators. I developed detailed lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes, focusing on features like design consistency, personalized recommendations, and efficiency.

This project emphasized enabling users to focus on content creation while saving time with AI-driven slide suggestions​

(2) GPTDashboard - Desktop View

The ChatGPT Dashboard was designed to help social media content creators streamline their workflow by integrating AI into content generation and tracking. I worked on both lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes, ensuring a smooth user experience across desktop and mobile views.

This project focused on addressing key pain points like burnout and content optimization while providing real-time feedback metrics to help creators stay on top of trends​

(3) SaveWatt

SaveWatt is a digital platform aimed at helping homeowners make smarter, energy-efficient appliance choices amidst rising electricity tariffs. I contributed to the design of a user-friendly interface that consolidates appliance comparison tools, offering personalized recommendations and energy-saving insights.

This project tackled the challenges of decision fatigue and financial strain by providing actionable guidance in a visually engaging way​

Full PortFolio: https://savewatt.framer.website

TeachSG

For my TeachSG attachment, I created this motivational card specifically for primary school students who were struggling with math. The card was designed to inspire confidence and encourage them as they prepared for their PSLE exams.

Using bright colors, cheerful designs, and a personalized touch with mentor memojis, I aimed to make the card relatable and uplifting, helping the students feel supported and motivated to achieve their best.

Video Editing and Production Work

Savvify - An Automated Savings Platform

During my time in Temasek Polytechnic, my group mates and I worked on an automated savings platform - Savvify. Essentially, the application helps users to round up your payment transactions to the nearest dollar, and crediting it to the users’ e-wallet upon making QR Payments.

Our main goal was to bring new solutions e-payment solutions while helping young adults like us, to understand real life responsibilities - improving their financial literacy!

Digital Storytelling

Pattoo Tarlour: The Art of the Needle

Here, we interviewed Pattoo Tarlour, a local tattoo business. We explored the artistry, passion, and personal stories behind tattooing in Singapore.

Through interviews with talented artists, visuals of their creative process, and dynamic shots capturing the parlour’s ambiance, the documentary highlights the unique culture and meaningful connections that define the space. It celebrates the cultural and artistic significance of tattooing as both a craft and an expression of identity.

Social Thought and Theory

In this module, we explored the contributions of the founding fathers of Sociology - Marx, Weber and Durkheim. This Social Media assignment looks into Weber’s ideas found in the The Protestant Work Ethic and the Spirit of Capitalism and Bureaucracy.

Here, we discussed (in a very Gen-Z way) that we have to be (economically) productive every single minute of our day, how we should be “growing money even in our sleep” is increasingly prevalent today. How everything we do now is to be justified in economic terms and we end up losing meaning, becoming disenchanted in life - Here’s what we did!
